About our Restaurant

Since our modest beginnings in 2005 with a little space in Toronto’s stylish Yorkville locale, ‘Organization Name’ ‘s development has been enlivened with the energy to cook and serve solid, Indian-roused takeout food. In contrast to other Indian eateries, ‘Organization Name’ was made with the explicit expectation to appear as something else. We realize numerous individuals love Indian sustenance, yet a large number of them loathe or are unconscious of the regularly unfortunate fixings that make run-of-the-mill Indian nourishment taste so great..
